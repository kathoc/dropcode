Dropcode

Text AirDrop, no app required.

Type or paste text, flash it as a QR code, and let another phone read it instantly with the default camera. On the flip side, Dropcode can also scan QR text straight from your device camera with no backend, no accounts, and no tracking.

Send Text

Anything you enter below is encoded directly into the QR code in real time, so another device can scan it and view the text immediately.

Static only: works on GitHub Pages, stores nothing, and uses your browser plus two CDN scripts.

Live QR

The QR code stays large for comfortable mobile scanning. Empty input shows a friendly prompt instead of a blank panel.

Scan this with a phone camera and the text will appear directly in the system QR preview.

Scan QR

Use your camera to decode QR text here. On phones, Dropcode asks for the rear camera when possible and stops scanning automatically after a successful read.

Camera idle.

Privacy

Dropcode is a tiny front-end-only utility. It does not upload text, write cookies, use analytics, or depend on any server logic.

If the browser blocks camera access or your device has no available camera, the scan controls hide and a clear message explains why.

No backend. No tracking. Just text in and text out.
